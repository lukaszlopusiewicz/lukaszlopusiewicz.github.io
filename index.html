About Me

I am an Associate Professor (Warsaw, Poland) and Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Greifswald (Germany), currently serving as a Senior Fellow at the Alfried Krupp Wissenschaftskolleg. My work focuses on fermentation of agri-food by-products, postbiotics, biofilm modulation, and sustainable packaging. In 2024, I was listed in the Stanford/Elsevier Top 2% most cited scientists worldwide for 2023.
